Support Our Mission

As a startup, our goal is to reduce the waste of community fruits and vegetables that the right people could enjoy. Often, homeowners with big fruit trees do not have the time or accessibility to pick their fruits, and a lot likely gets wasted and stays on the trees. By giving homeowners the opportunity to donate their fruit, either by them picking it for us or our volunteers picking it for them, we aim to reduce food wastage and help people in need
